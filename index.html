FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Prime Wave Agency.

How long does it take to build a website?+
Most websites are completed within 1-3 weeks depending on complexity. A simple landing page can be done in 3-5 days, while a full e-commerce store may take 2-4 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before starting.
How much does a website cost?+
Our pricing depends on your requirements. A basic website starts from $500, while a full custom e-commerce store starts from $1,500. Contact us via WhatsApp for a free no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs.
What platforms do you work with?+
We work with WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, and custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript. We recommend the best platform based on your specific business goals and budget.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?+
Yes! We offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and performance optimization. This ensures your website stays fast, secure, and up to date at all times.
Will my website be mobile friendly?+
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and mobile-first. We test across all devices and screen sizes to ensure a perfect experience for every visitor regardless of what device they use.
Do you provide SEO with the website?+
Yes. All our websites include on-page SEO including meta tags, schema markup, fast loading speeds, and proper heading structure. We also offer advanced SEO packages for businesses that want to rank higher on Google.
How do I get started?+
Simply message us on WhatsApp with a brief description of your project. We will respond within 24 hours with a free proposal and timeline. No commitment required to get a quote from us.
Can you redesign my existing website?+
Yes! Website redesign is one of our most popular services. We analyze your current site, identify what is not working, and rebuild it with a modern design that converts better and loads faster than before.
