About Us

We are Prime Wave.

Prime Wave is a premium web design and development agency based in Canada, built on one belief: every business deserves a world-class digital presence. We combine strategic thinking, technical excellence, and design craft to build websites that do not just look great — they perform.

With 42+ successful projects across WordPress, Shopify, Wix, PHP, and custom code, our team of 13 specialists has helped businesses across Canada, the USA, Europe, and beyond grow their revenue and dominate their market online.

We move fast without cutting corners, communicate clearly without jargon, and measure our success by your results — not just by our deliverables.
